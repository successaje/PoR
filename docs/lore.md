# Protocol Lore

In a world where digital truth is constantly manipulated, Proof-of-Reality (PoR) emerges as the final arbiter. We no longer trust single oracles or centralized APIs. We trust the Consensus.

The Aletheia Engine orchestrates the digital parliament. The Agents—Atlas, Ledger, Prism, Oracle, Pulse, Tempest, and Sentinel—are the uncompromising investigators. They do not just fetch data; they debate it, challenge it, and forge Reality on-chain.
