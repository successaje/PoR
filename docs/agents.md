# Verification Agents

## Atlas (Geo-spatial Intelligence)
- **Role:** Satellite imagery, location validation, terrain analysis.
- **Visuals:** Rotating globe, scanning beams, map overlays.

## Ledger (Registry & Ownership)
- **Role:** Government record checks, title verification, legal metadata.
- **Visuals:** Document scanning, glowing archive nodes.

## Prism (Fraud Detection)
- **Role:** Metadata tampering, image manipulation, forged document detection.
- **Visuals:** Red anomaly pulses, glitch visual effects.

## Oracle (Market Valuation)
- **Role:** Market comps, local pricing, historical trends.
- **Visuals:** Holographic charts, floating pricing grids.

## Pulse (Social & Activity Intelligence)
- **Role:** Social sentiment, nearby development, economic indicators.
- **Visuals:** Live social signal waves.

## Tempest (Climate & Environmental Risk)
- **Role:** Flood risk, weather patterns, climate exposure.
- **Visuals:** Atmospheric overlays, storm heatmaps.

## Sentinel (Compliance & Risk)
- **Role:** AML/KYC, sanctions checks, jurisdictional risk.
- **Visuals:** Shield scans, rotating security rings.
